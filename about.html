About EMI

Ethnic Manufacturers of India – Powering the Future of Men's Ethnic Wear

Who We Are

Ethnic Manufacturers of India (EMI) is a leading organization based in Mumbai, dedicated to bringing together the finest men's ethnic wear manufacturers across India. EMI creates a unified platform where brands, designers, and retailers collaborate, grow, and innovate.

With a strong presence in the industry, EMI organizes large-scale exhibitions, connects businesses, and drives growth in the ethnic fashion segment.

Our Vision

To build India’s most powerful platform for ethnic wear manufacturers and take Indian craftsmanship to a global level.

Our Mission

Connecting manufacturers, retailers, and designers while promoting innovation, quality, and collaboration in ethnic fashion.
